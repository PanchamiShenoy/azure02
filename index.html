ABOUT AVENUE SALON


Avenue Salon was founded in May, 2007 with the vision to bring together some of the area’s best hair stylists who enjoy spending time with clients as much as they enjoy creating hair masterpieces. There are no attitudes to contend with here. The Avenue team is happy to consult with you directly to make sure you get the style you want, and have a great experience the whole way.

Our team has the diversity of knowledge, interests and styles to help you achieve the look you want and overcome any hair problems that get in the way. Stop by the salon to say “hello”, or call us today to schedule an appointment with any of our stylists.
